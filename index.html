Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

The Peace Lily, scientifically known as Spathiphyllum, is a popular indoor plant prized for its elegant foliage and unique white flowers. Belonging to the Araceae family, this tropical plant is native to the rainforests of Central and South America. Its botanical name, Spathiphyllum, is derived from Greek words meaning "spathe" (a type of flower bract) and "leaf."

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

The ZZ plant, formally known as Zamioculcas zamiifolia, is a striking tropical plant native to Eastern Africa. Its distinct appearance and adaptability make it a favorite among indoor plant enthusiasts. The plant is recognized for its thick, shiny, dark green leaves that grow in a graceful, arching manner, giving it an appealing and ornamental appearance.

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

The Pothos, scientifically named Epipremnum aureum, is a versatile and resilient tropical plant native to the Solomon Islands. It is celebrated for its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and decorative spaces.
